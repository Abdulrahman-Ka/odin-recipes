Lasagna

Description

It's homemade recipe with a meaty, made from scratch tomato sauce and deliciously cheesy filing

It's usually serves with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
