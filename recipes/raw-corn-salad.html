Raw Corn Salad

Description

Quick, healthy, and tasty raw corn salad for the summer. Uses fresh, raw, sweet corn on the cop, avocado.

Ingredients

  • 2 ears corn, kernels cut from the cob
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 small sweet onion
  • 1 green onion, green parts only
  • 1 drizzle extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 lime
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mix corn kernels, tomatoes, cucumber, avocado, onion, cilantro, and green onion.
  2. Add a drizzle of olive oil.
  3. Squeeze lime juice into the bowl.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
