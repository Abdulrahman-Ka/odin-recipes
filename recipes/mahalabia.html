Mahalabia

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 4 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Combine milk, cream, sugar, vanilla sugar, and cornstarch in saucepan and whisk until well combined.
  2. Slowly bring to a boil over medium heat and cook, stirring constantly, until thickened, 10 to 15 minutes.
  3. pour pudding into 4 dessert bowls and allow to cool to room temperature.
  4. Refrigerate until chilled, about an hour, sprinkle with cinnamon to serve.
